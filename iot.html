Internet of Things

B.Tech in Computer Science & Engineering (Cyber Security) creates wide range of careers for students in the emerging trends. The programme equips students with the knowledge, skills and abilities in creating security and protecting organization’s e-resources, information and networks in a system enterprise environment. The rising number of the computer Network (internet/intranet) attacks and complexity in attack technologies has made the emergence of this area of study as an opportunity for research and pursuing careers in the industry.

Many reputed companies recruit individuals who have knowledge and skills to stop these attacks and make organizations’ networks more robust and secured.

The department has qualified faculty members with strong expertise in the research areas of Data Science, Artificial intelligence, Natural Language Processing, Cyber Security systems, software engineering, data base systems and data warehousing systems, information systems and computer networking. Industry-institute interaction is a regular phenomenon of Computer Science & Engineering departments.
