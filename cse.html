CSE Department

The department has qualified faculty with expertise in research areas of Software Engineering, Data Base Systems and Data Warehousing Systems, Information Systems and Computer Networking.

Industry-institute interaction is a regular phenomenon of Computer Science & Engineering department. Workshops and seminars on different technological aspects and emerging trends are conducted at regular intervals to keep students updated with industry requirements.

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering was established during year 2004.The Department offers B.Tech Programme in Computer Science and Engineering for Regular &lateral entry students. The Department has well established labs which facilitate students to experiment with computing challenges and develop solutions using emerging technologies.

The Department has Professors with Ph.D & few staff members pursuing their Ph.D. The department has a strong team of close to 30 faculty members with expertise and research interest in diversified areas of Computer Science & Engineering.





Head of the Department

Dr. J. MANORANJINI

Dr. J.Manoranjini has completed Btech, Mtech, Ph.D. from Anna University, in the field of Computer Science & Engineering. She is a good contributor in Academics. She has a vast experience of over 15 years in the domains of teaching and research. She is currently the Head of CSE and Cyber Security in “Swami Vivekananda Institute of Technology”, Secunderabad and she is basically a computer science engineer and previously held academic positions at Anna University affiliated colleges.



She has been evaluator for toycathon and Hackathon programs organized by Ministry of Innovation & education. She has conducted AICTE National Conference NECCC 21. She was invited by colleges like Malla reddy Institute of technology,Vignan institute of Technology and many colleges as guest speaker.

She has completed courses in Machine Learning, ethical hacking, cyber forensics. Her current research interests in the areas of : Adhoc networks , computer networks , wireless sensor networks and cyber security . She has published more than 20 papers in these areas and got several credits for outstanding publications in renowned journals like Springer & Elsevier .

She has been guiding more than 50 projects in UG & PG level Dr.J.Manoranjini has also written a book in Cloud Computing, Operating system and her chapter " Data Security and Privacy Preserving in Edge Computing : Cryptography and Trust management systems " in the emerging technological book named " Cases on Edge Computing and Analytics ", is published by IGI Global International Academic publisher.And she has also written books “Machine learning Techniques” and “Cyber Forensics”.

Her eminent contribution in the field of computer science and her thirst for new knowledge has been appreciated by the International association of Engineers ( IAENG ) and she is a part of the engineering society.

She plays a key role in empowering both her team and students in keeping them abreast with emerging technological advancements. Her core interests lie in research & development and enjoys enjoys teaching varied conceptual programs to the student at various levels.

Good public speaking skills,singer,dancer,state level sports – representatives Tamilnadu in womens Hockey team. She also motivates and supports students by counseling them to achieve their goals.
